Meat Lasagna Recipe

Cooking Instructions

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C) and prepare your workspace.

In a large skillet, cook the Italian sausage and ground beef over medium heat.

Add the minced onion and crushed garlic to the meat mixture.

Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce.

Add water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, and Italian seasoning.

Season with salt and pepper, then let simmer for 30 minutes.

Meanwhile, bring a large pot of salted water to boil.

Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions.

In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg and parsley.

Drain the cooked noodles and set aside on a clean kitchen towel.

Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.

Place 3 noodles lengthwise over the sauce in the baking dish.

Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.

Top with half of the mozzarella cheese slices.

Spoon half of the remaining meat sauce over the mozzarella.

Repeat layers once more, ending with remaining noodles on top.

Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.

Remove foil and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

Bake uncovered for an additional 25 minutes until bubbly.

Let stand for 15 minutes before serving to allow layers to set.
